# FBS Distribution Strategy
*The undefeated distribution strategy — from one founding pastor to 100 churches*

## The Single Insight

> Pastors don't trust vendors — they trust other pastors. Your pastor-investor is worth more than a $50k ad budget. Every tactic below flows from this.

| Stat | Number |

## Phase 1 — Months 0–3: Prove It

**Goal: Win your founding churches**

### Your pastor is your anchor — Do this first
His $5–10k investment + 5–10 church intros is the entire go-to-market seed. Make his church the flagship case study. Document every metric: engagement up, giving up, attendance retention. That data is the sales deck.

### Founding church pricing — High ROI
Offer 3–5 churches a founding rate ($199–299/mo, locked forever) in exchange for:
- Testimonials
- Referrals
- A video case study

These 5 churches are worth more than 50 cold leads.

### Nail the demo experience — Essential
Pastors don't buy software — they buy outcomes. Demo with:
- Their church name
- Their sermon series already loaded
- Their congregation size in the metrics

Personalization wins every time in this market.

---

## Phase 2 — Months 3–9: Multiply

**Goal: Activate the pastor network flywheel**

### Denomination network penetration — The multiplier
Get endorsed by one denomination's regional director = warm access to hundreds of churches overnight.

Target denominations:
- Assemblies of God
- Southern Baptist
- Non-denom megachurch networks

One endorsement = ~50 warm leads.

### Pastor conference presence — Fast trust
Be at 2–3 pastor/church conferences per year. Not a booth — a demo table or speaking slot.
- Exponential Conference
- Catalyst Conference
- Local denominational gatherings

This is how Tithe.ly and Subsplash grew.

### Pastor affiliate program — Self-distributing
Pay pastors $50–100/month recurring for every church they refer that stays subscribed.
- A well-connected pastor earns $500–1,000/mo passively
- Turns customers into the sales force
- Tool: Rewardful + Stripe

### Christian influencer / pastor YouTube — Mid-term
Target pastors with 5k–100k YouTube subscribers who talk about church growth. Offer a free account + affiliate deal. One video from a trusted pastor voice can drive 20–50 demo requests. The Cal AI playbook for church tech.

---

## Phase 3 — Months 9+: Scale

**Goal: Build owned distribution**

### SEO + content for church leaders — Compounding
Target search queries like:
- "How to increase church attendance between Sundays"
- "Best church app 2025"

A blog that genuinely helps church leaders drives inbound leads at near-zero cost. Tithe.ly built this well.

### Church leader community (Slack / Facebook group) — Plays long
Run a free community around the 'faith beyond Sunday' movement. When they trust FBS as a thought leader, converting them to paying customers is effortless. This is the moat Subsplash never built.

### Paid ads (Facebook/Google) — Not yet
Ads work in church tech but only after a proven case study and sharp messaging. Running ads cold to a $499/mo product with no social proof = wasted budget.
- Save for month 12+
- Exception: retargeting website visitors

## Key Reminders

### What will NOT work
- Cold email blasts
- LinkedIn outreach to "Church Administrator"
- Generic Facebook ads without a case study
- Door knocking
- Cold calling from a purchased list

### The path to 100 churches
The path to 100 churches isn't 100 individual sales conversations. It's **4–5 deeply trusted relationships that each unlock 20–25 churches organically.**
