## Bugs Fixed This Session

- **Locked badge gray shades were inconsistent** ‚Äî `filter: grayscale(1)` converts each badge's unique color to a different gray shade. Fixed by using a flat `hsl(var(--muted))` background on `LockedBadgeCard` instead.
- **Lock icon overlapping text** ‚Äî was centered absolutely over the card. Fixed by making it the last flex item (right-aligned).
- **Founding Member shown as locked** ‚Äî can't be earned after signup. Fixed by excluding it from `specialLocked`.
- **`bible_reads` not logging** ‚Äî RLS INSERT policy was missing initially (already existed from prior session as it turned out).
- **Badge banner never firing** ‚Äî `reflection_badges` was not in the `supabase_realtime` publication. The channel had two listeners; the missing table broke the whole channel. Fixed by running `ALTER PUBLICATION supabase_realtime ADD TABLE reflection_badges`.
